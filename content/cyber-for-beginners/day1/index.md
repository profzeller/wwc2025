# Day 1 — Foundations of Cybersecurity

## How to use this day
- Use the left navigation to move through topics and labs in order.
- Pages are designed to support discussion and instructor facilitation.
- Labs are small-group friendly and portable for instructors.

## Learning outcomes
By the end of Day 1, participants will be able to:
- Explain what information security is and why it matters across IT disciplines
- Apply the CIA Triad and risk thinking to real-world incidents
- Identify threats, vulnerabilities, and controls
- Describe foundational network/web/identity concepts from a security lens
- Explain why logging and incident response are essential

## Suggested pacing (long day)
- Welcome + framing (30–45 min)
- InfoSec + CIA + Lab 1 (90–120 min)
- Risk + threats/vulns/controls (60–75 min)
- Hygiene + Lab 2 + layered defense (90–120 min)
- Networking + web + identity (90–120 min)
- Crypto + logging + incident response (75–105 min)
- Wrap-up (15–30 min)
