## How to Use This Day

- Use the left navigation to move through topics and labs in order  
- Pages are designed to support **discussion, not just reading**
- Lab pages are intended for small-group or facilitated activities
- Reflection prompts may be used verbally or as written exercises

Day 1 establishes a shared foundation for understanding cybersecurity concepts,
language, and teaching approaches.

This day focuses on **how to think about cybersecurity**, not how to master tools.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of Day 1, participants will be able to:

- **Explain** what information security is and why it matters across IT disciplines
- **Apply** the CIA Triad to real-world cybersecurity incidents
- **Identify** common threats, vulnerabilities, and controls
- **Apply** foundational cyber hygiene practices in teachable ways
