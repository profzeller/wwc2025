# Pre-Assessment (Baseline)

## What this is
This is a quick, low-stakes check of what you already know. It helps you (and your instructor) see growth over time.

## Instructions (10 minutes)
Answer quickly. If you are not sure, write “Not sure yet.” Do not look things up.

## Questions

1. In your own words, define:
   - **Confidentiality**
   - **Integrity**
   - **Availability**
2. What is the difference between a **threat** and a **vulnerability**?
3. What does **least privilege** mean?
4. What does **multi-factor authentication (MFA)** protect against? What does it *not* protect against?
5. What is the difference between **hashing** and **encryption**?
6. List three common warning signs of a **phishing** message.
7. If you think a security incident is happening, what should you do first (before trying to “fix” it)?
8. What is a **log**? Why do organizations keep logs?
